Buddy Sensei is a **web application powered by artificial intelligence**, designed to help students **create academic content quickly and in a personalized way**. Whether it's writing summaries, generating project ideas, or structuring presentations, Buddy Sensei streamlines the creation process while adapting to each user's specific needs.

---

## Objective 🎯

The goal of Buddy Sensei was to **reduce students' cognitive load** by automating repetitive tasks related to academic content creation. The application aimed to improve productivity, facilitate understanding, and provide intelligent support for learning.

---

## My Role and Incubation 🚀

The project started in May 2023, and in 2024 Buddy Sensei was selected to participate in the **Y’ello Startup program by MTN Côte d'Ivoire**, an incubator dedicated to innovative digital startups. Although the project **was not selected as a winner**, the experience was extremely rewarding: mentorship, technical resources, and visibility allowed the team to strengthen the product and refine our approach.

As a founding member, I was involved in:

- **Designing the user interface** to ensure a smooth and intuitive experience.
- **Defining key features** in collaboration with target users.
- **Coordinating with technical partners** for integrating AI into the application.

This stage was crucial for **gaining my first skills in incubation, product management, and SaaS design**.

---

## Current Status and Outlook 🔮

Buddy Sensei is currently in the **pre-launch phase**. The application will soon be available to students, with features such as:

- **Automatic summary generation** from documents or lecture notes.
- **Creation of personalized revision plans** based on subjects and deadlines.
- **Writing assistance** for essays and presentations with contextual suggestions.

The goal is to **launch Buddy Sensei on the Ivorian market** and expand its accessibility to other French-speaking African countries, collaborating with educational institutions and partner organizations.

---

## Expected Impact 🌍

- **Improve student productivity** by reducing the time spent on content creation.
- **Promote equal opportunities** by providing advanced learning tools accessible to all.
- **Contribute to the digital transformation of education** in Africa by integrating technological solutions tailored to local needs.

This project has been one of the most challenging of my career and has taught me to **use technology to solve real-world problems** and to understand how to **support students in their academic journey**, while learning from formative experiences even when you don't win.
