### The Context

In a world saturated with information, the modern student is paradoxically more helpless in the face of their courses.

The accumulation of PDFs, videos, and scattered notes creates **"cognitive noise"** that hinders memorization and deep reflection. Buddy Sensei was born from this desire to transform this chaos into clarity.

### The Challenge

The idea was not to create yet another chatbot, but an assistant capable of deciphering the structure of a course.

We identified three critical pain points:
- **Paralysis** in the face of massive volumes of data.
- **Disorientation** when synthesizing complex concepts.
- **Lack of interactive and personalized support** during revisions.

### The Design Approach

For Buddy Sensei, I designed an interface that "breathes" to soothe exam-related anxiety.

The major innovation lies in **intent-based guidance**. Rather than leaving the user with an empty field, we offer contextual actions:
1. **Extract** key concepts.
2. **Generate** a structured revision plan.
3. **Test** knowledge via dynamic quizzes.

### Impact & Vision

After three years of evolution, Buddy Sensei has grown from a prototype into a daily tool for thousands of learners.

The most rewarding feedback highlights our success: *"Buddy doesn't do my homework for me, it helps me understand how to do it better."* This is the very essence of the project: technology that **amplifies human intelligence** without substituting for it.
