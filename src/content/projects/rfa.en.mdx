Femme Avenir Network is the official website of this NGO dedicated to **highlighting the journeys and ambitions of women** in Côte d'Ivoire. As a member, I was able to follow and contribute to the promotion of this mission: presenting the vision, actions, and history of the organization in a clear and accessible way.

---

## Challenge & Vision 🎯

How do you design a website that is both **informative and inspiring**, allowing every visitor to understand the scope and impact of the NGO? The goal was to create a clear and intuitive interface to **showcase the vision, history, and key initiatives**, while reflecting the humanitarian and supportive spirit of the community.

---

## Process & Approach 🔧

- **Immersion in the mission**: As a member, I identified the essential content to highlight (history, projects, actions, testimonials).
- **Information structuring**: Clear hierarchy and smooth navigation so that every visitor can easily explore the NGO’s mission and initiatives.
- **Visual design**: A simple and elegant interface, with colors and typography chosen to reflect **credibility, humanity, and commitment**.

---

## Results 🚀

- A website that **effectively presents the vision and history of the NGO**.
- Clear showcasing of the projects and actions led by Femme Avenir Network.
- A platform that inspires trust and highlights the humanitarian spirit: **"Giving much more than what we possess."**

Every design element was created to **support the NGO’s mission** and showcase the work accomplished over the years.
