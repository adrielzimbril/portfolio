### The Context

Réseau Femme Avenir (RFA) is a quiet force on the ground in Ivory Coast.

Since 2018, the NGO has transformed the lives of over **500 women**. Yet, despite this massive impact, the organization suffered from "digital silence." This invisibility on the web acted as a glass ceiling, limiting access to international funding.

### The Challenge

As an active member of the NGO, I had a mission of trust: translating the organization's soul into a rigorous digital experience.

The challenge was to:
- **Build credibility** for field action among donors.
- **Tell** human stories without falling into melodrama.
- **Convert** simple curiosity into engagement (donations, partnerships).

### Our Approach: The Design of Sincerity

I designed an interface where **White Space** plays a crucial role: it lets essential messages breathe.

We structured the journey to answer partners' critical questions:
1. **Identity**: Who carries the mission?
2. **Action**: What are the concrete and verifiable results?
3. **Engagement**: How to become a link in the chain?

The integration of an **"Impact Dashboard"** on the home page allowed cold statistics to be transformed into a palpable human adventure.

### Impact & Results

The site acted as a strategic catalyst.

Beyond aesthetics, it opened major institutional doors:
- **Traffic**: 340% increase in organic visits.
- **Reach**: Internationalization of the audience (Europe, North America).
- **Professionalism**: Cited by auditors as a guarantee of transparency.

Today, RFA has a showcase equal to its ambitions, where excellence is combined with the human element.
