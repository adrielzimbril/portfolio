ShiroGifts was my **first SaaS project**, born from the desire to **allow users to share their streaming subscriptions and reduce their costs**, while earning a bit of money by sharing their unused subscriptions. Netflix, Amazon Prime, Disney+, and many others could be monetized in a simple and secure way.

This project was **the starting point of my passion for SaaS**, introduced me to the world of digital products, and allowed me to acquire concrete skills that I still use today.

---

## Concept and goal 🎯

The goal was to create a **simple, intuitive, and secure platform** where each user could:

- Create or join groups to share a subscription.
- Reduce their expenses while earning money.
- Enjoy a smooth and clear interface, focused on user experience.

The aim: to turn an everyday need into an economic opportunity, while maintaining control and security.

---

## Journey & learnings 📅

Active from **December 2021 to December 2024**, ShiroGifts allowed me to:

- Discover **the entire lifecycle of a SaaS product**, from idea to launch.
- Strengthen my skills in **UX/UI design**, organization, and user-centered design.
- Understand **user behaviors and the economic mechanics of a digital product**.

Although the SaaS was discontinued for certain reasons, it was a **true learning laboratory**, where every product decision prepared me for my future projects.

---

## Impact 🚀

- First real experience of **launching a SaaS with a concrete economic impact**.
- A deep understanding of how a digital product can meet financial and social needs.
- Solid foundation for my current expertise in **Product Design and SaaS launches**.
