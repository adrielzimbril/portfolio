### The Context

In 2020, with the lockdowns, streaming consumption exploded. Paradoxically, the cumulative cost of subscriptions became a financial burden for many.

Although **70% of users** were already sharing their accounts, they were doing so in an artisanal and risky manner. ShiroGifts was born from an intuition: industrialize this sharing to make it as fluid and secure as a direct subscription.

### The Challenge

The greatest challenge was not technical, but **psychological**.

How to reassure a customer buying a "shared access"? We had to build an interface that breathes legitimacy and security, the opposite of dark piracy sites. The stakes were clear:
- **Reassure** about the longevity of the access.
- **Simplify** the recovery of credentials.
- **Automate** the management of technical disputes.

### Our Solution: Managed-Sharing

I iterated on several models to finally create **"Managed-Sharing"**. ShiroGifts became the sole guarantor of the transaction:
1. **Confidentiality**: The user deals only with the platform, never with other members.
2. **Instant Delivery**: An optimized 3-click purchase funnel with immediate delivery.
3. **Centralized Dashboard**: A "My Account" section allowing users to retrieve their access in the blink of an eye.

The design system was thought out for speed, guaranteeing an ultra-fluid experience even on unstable mobile connections.

### End of Adventure & Legacy

After 3 years of activity and over **6,500 customers served**, I made the decision to close ShiroGifts in 2024. The evolution of the legal framework and pressure from platforms made the model too uncertain at scale.

However, this project remains my most formative experience. It taught me how to manage exponential growth and proved that **excellent UX** can transform an informal practice into a premium user experience.
