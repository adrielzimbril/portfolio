Deewid is an **innovative e-commerce platform**. It enables entrepreneurs to quickly create professional online stores, while offering simplified management of products, orders, and payments.

---

## Objective 🎯

Deewid's goal is to **democratize access to e-commerce** in Africa by providing a turnkey solution for merchants who want to grow their online presence. The platform combines simplicity, flexibility, and features tailored to African markets.

---

## My Role and Incubation 🚀

In 2024, Deewid was selected to participate in the **Y’ello Startup program by MTN Côte d'Ivoire**, an incubator for innovative digital startups. This experience allowed me to:

- **Design the user interface** for a smooth and intuitive experience.
- **Define key features** in collaboration with users.
- **Coordinate with technical partners** for product development.

This experience was crucial for **gaining skills in UX/UI, project management, and product development**, while understanding the specific needs of the African market.

---

## Perspectives 🔮

Deewid offers entrepreneurs:

- **Creation of customizable online stores**.
- **Management of products, orders, and payments**.
- **Integration of local payment solutions**.
- **Multichannel customer support**.

---

## Impact 🌍

Deewid makes e-commerce more accessible for African entrepreneurs, boosts the growth of small businesses, and contributes to the digital transformation of commerce with solutions tailored to local needs.
