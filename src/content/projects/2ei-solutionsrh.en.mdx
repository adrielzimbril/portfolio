Every project starts with a simple question: how can we make the experience for HR professionals, candidates, and managers smoother and more enjoyable?\
For 2EI HR Solutions, it was not just about designing a web application, but about rethinking user journeys to reduce friction and improve overall satisfaction.

---

## Process & Approach 🔧

**Explore, test, iterate.**

- **Immersion**: analysis of existing usage and interviews with key users.
- **Co-creation**: collaborative workshops with internal teams to align needs and product vision.
- **Journey analysis**: UX audits of existing interfaces to identify obstacles.
- **Collaborative workshops**: sessions with HR teams to understand real needs and align the product vision.
- **Wireframes and prototypes**: iterative solutions tested with users.
- **Final design**: clear, intuitive, and consistent interfaces, aligned with business and user objectives.

---

## Result 🚀

The project led to a smoother and more intuitive platform:

- HR professionals complete their tasks more quickly.
- Candidates apply without confusion or frustration.
- Managers track recruitment efficiently.

Thanks to this approach, user satisfaction increased and the overall experience became more consistent and enjoyable.
