### The Context

2EI Solutions RH is a central platform for recruitment actors.

Despite its functional richness, the interface suffered from a major problem: the accumulation of unfiltered information. This created **decision fatigue** among recruiters and anxiety among candidates.

### The Challenge

The challenge was not only graphic; it was structural.

The audit revealed that most frictions came from:
- Inconsistent status nomenclature.
- A glaring lack of visual hierarchy.
- Recruiters spending 30% of their time managing manual follow-ups.

### Our Approach

We started with a total deconstruction of the journeys to define **"intent dashboards"**.

1. **For the recruiter**: The priority is validation.
2. **For the candidate**: The priority is visibility.

I implemented a dynamic status badge system, coupled with cleaner typography (**Outfit**) to improve visual scanning. Each screen was scrutinized through Hick's Law: reducing noise to amplify action.

### Results & Impact

The transformation was immediate. By making the journeys predictable, we relieved the operational teams:

- **Time saving**: Identification of critical actions 4x faster.
- **Support reduction**: Spectacular drop in unnecessary follow-ups (from 60% to 15%).

The average time to identify a status went from **12 to 3 seconds**. Recruiters can now focus on the human element.
