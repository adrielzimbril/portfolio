# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how I collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use my portfolio website, community features, and related services. As a Product & UI/UX Designer, I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about my data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account on my portfolio, I collect:

- Your name and email address
- Profile information (avatar, bio)
- Authentication credentials (handled securely through GitHub, Google, etc.)
- Any content you choose to display in the community

### Community Activity

When you participate in the community wall or design challenges:

- Messages and comments you post
- Challenge submissions and project links
- Reactions and engagement data
- Anonymous user identifiers for unauthenticated users

### Technical Data

I automatically collect:

- IP address and browser information
- Device and usage statistics
- Page views and interaction patterns
- Performance and analytics data

## How I Use Your Information

I use your information to:

- Provide and maintain my portfolio platform
- Enable community features and design challenges
- Process challenge submissions and evaluations
- Send notifications about updates
- Analyze usage patterns to improve my services
- Prevent abuse and ensure platform security

## Authentication Providers

I use third-party authentication providers (GitHub, Google) for account creation and login. These providers may collect and process your personal information according to their own privacy policies. I only receive the information necessary to create and maintain your account.

## Community Content

- Messages posted on the community wall are public
- Challenge submissions may be displayed publicly for evaluation
- Your profile information is visible to other users
- You can delete or modify your content at any time through your account settings

## Cookies and Tracking

I use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Keep you logged in to your account
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze platform usage and performance
- Improve user experience

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

## Third-Party Services

I may share your information with:

- **Authentication providers** (GitHub, Google) for account management
- **Analytics services** to understand platform usage
- **Hosting providers** to deliver the service
- **Email services** for communication (if you opt-in)

I do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Data Retention

- Account information is retained while your account is active
- Community content is retained until you delete it
- Analytics data is retained for platform improvement purposes
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and data
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Export your data

## Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication flows
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

## Children's Privacy

My portfolio platform is not intended for users under 13 years of age. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify users of significant changes through the platform.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or my data practices, please contact me at hello@adrielzimbril.com. For data deletion requests (for example, to remove your messages from the community wall), you can also use this email address.
