A sense of **harmony, integrity, and serenity** is deeply woven into Japanese website design.

Even when a project draws its flawless appearance from extravagant visuals or is driven by cutting-edge features and dynamic effects that might otherwise introduce chaos and disrupt the balance, it always finds the perfect equilibrium between content and design. The result is the impression that every element is an integral building block of the composition.

This unity and tranquil aesthetic can be traced to an urban understanding of Zen, which establishes a peaceful and relaxed atmosphere. Such projects, with their almost ethereal overall feel, deliver a truly exquisite user experience.

These 20 fresh examples from talented Japanese web designers and developers should give you a clear sense of the unique characteristics inherent in Japanese websites.

## Best Japanese Websites

### Kenji Saito

![Exit Film](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356551-Kenji-Saito.jpg)

Kenji Saito demonstrates to visitors the full power hidden within HTML5/CSS3/JS. This talented developer’s personal portfolio is filled with fantastic experimental works that can convince anyone the artist is “like a duck to water” in this field.

### Exit Film

[![Exit Film](https://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/2-Exit-Film.jpg)](https://exitfilm.jp/)

Exit Film successfully places four short videos on users’ radar in a non-intrusive way. The elegant fixed-centered layout, paired with a dark, solid background, tiny graphics, and delicate typography, creates a strong visual impact without overwhelming visitors.

### Satoshi Kuroda

![Satoshi Kuroda](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356563-Satoshi-Kuroda.jpg)

The first thing you notice is the splendid brush typography that stands out beautifully against the background. Not only does the lettering highlight the artist’s creative nature, but it also serves as a powerful decorative element that completes the overall look.

### Kenji Endo

![Kenji Endo](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356564-Kenji-Endo.jpg)

Kenji Endo’s official site skillfully showcases the artist. The elegant combination of black and white enhances the aesthetic, blessing the project with a stunning and memorable look. Additionally, a few subtle complementary effects give the site a positive vibe.

### Gakuten

![Gakuten](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356575-Gakuten.jpg)

Gakuten is a summer art festival that infuses its brilliant personality into its official website, closely tying it to the event. The homepage, as it should, features spectacular photos, team members, and crucial information for those interested in participating.

### Sanga Ryokan

[![Sanga Ryokan](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356576-Sanga-Ryokan.jpg)](https://www.sanga-ryokan.com/)

Sanga Ryokan boasts a beautiful online portfolio with a strong Japanese atmosphere. While the landing page design subtly recalls yin and yang through clever background differentiation, the modular scrolling technique allows users to scan each column individually, elegantly splitting the website into two parts.

### Hys-inc

[![Hys-inc](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356577-Hys-inc.jpg)](https://www.hys-inc.jp/)

Hys-inc opts for a cleaner, pure, and pristine look that certainly catches the eye and easily contrasts with the foreground elements. The artist has carefully balanced graphics, content, and animations to enrich the website and clearly demonstrate their area of expertise.

### Koto-bana

[![Koto-bana](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356578-Koto-bana.jpg)](https://koto-bana.com/)

If you’re searching for pioneering concepts or innovative ideas, explore this site. Koto-bana is a fantastic gallery capable of giving your inspiration a significant boost. Using modern techniques, it draws flowers from words. It’s both interesting and captivating.

### Lexus

[![Lexus](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356589-Beyond.jpg)](https://discoverlexus.com/stories)

Beyond impresses with its stunning photographic background, setting the tone for innovation. The online magazine skillfully combines the serenity of a seascape with the luxury of a Lexus car, featuring a truly dramatic image that, like a magnet, draws visitors’ eyes.

### Don-gouri

![Don-gouri](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663565810-Don-guri.jpg)

Don-guri offers a great way to lose track of time for a few minutes. This music-inspired project fills your workspace with positive emotions, brightens the mood, helps you escape your troubles, and is sure to make your day. It charms not only with its original idea but also with its bright and joyful execution.

### Hanatsubaki

[![Hanatsubaki](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663565912-Howlt.jpg)](https://hanatsubaki.shiseido.com/jp/)

Impressive photo manipulation gives the website a sense of personality and exudes an image of majestic beauty. While custom photography takes center stage, the perfectly balanced design is based on an agreed tandem of angular blocks and modest coloring for a strong effect.

### Howlt

[![Howlt](https://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/12-Howlt.jpg)](https://howlt.com/archives/artworks)

The designer skillfully blends doodles and animations, easily setting this personal portfolio apart from others and allowing it to speak for itself. The cartoon theme highlights the artworks seamlessly and adds to the site’s creativity and artistry.

### Fabrik

![Fabrik](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663565913-Fabrik.jpg)

The artist prefers to get straight to the point and showcase the fabrics in the best possible light using beautiful close-ups. Thanks to a light color palette, rigid layout, and generous spacing, the product naturally becomes the focal point.

### Kobaphoto

![Kobaphoto](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566014-Kobaphoto.jpg)

Kobaphoto is a personal portfolio where the images speak for the photographer. The typography and navigation do not distract from the masterpieces, smoothly shifting the focus to the visuals.

### Don-gouri Magazine

![Don-gouri Magazine](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566015-Don-guri-Magazine.jpg)

This is another project from the Don-guri team in our collection that deserves attention. It makes the most of one of the most iconic elements of modern Japanese culture—anime. It doesn’t feature vibrant animations, but it’s executed in a juicy and distinctive style that’s sure to spark interest.

### Letters

[![Letters](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566116-letters-inc.jpg)](https://www.letters-inc.jp/)

Monochrome, delicate graphics in a line-art style, animated by tiny accompanying effects, brighten up the Letters.inc homepage. The resemblance to the grotesque posters of “The Great Gatsby” adds a touch of exquisite beauty to the landing page.

### Ondo

![Ondo](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566117-Ondo.jpg)

Ondo’s homepage design definitely stands out from the crowd. Unconventional color schemes, whimsical animations, and unique components evoke mixed feelings, sure to leave a mark on users’ minds.

### Midori Aoyama

![Midori Aoyama](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566118-Midori-Aoyama.jpg)

Midori Aoyama has infused their personal portfolio with high-tech energy. Every element contributes to the theme: delicate typography, ghost buttons, a vibrant background, and even a self-portrait with a beautiful low-poly illustrated touch.

### Herbal Bises

![Herbal Bises](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566119-Herbal-Bises.jpg)

Herbal Bises’ homepage is marked by incredible photo manipulation that reflects an undeniable sense of naturalness. The sumptuous centerpiece not only draws all the attention but also maintains harmony and enriches the design.

### Red Bull

![Red Bull](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566220-redbull.jpg)

The artist turns to modern solutions to efficiently handle multimedia, animate content, strengthen the brand, and deliver an incredible user experience on the website.

**Conclusion**

Japanese artists are completely at ease with cutting-edge techniques and current trends. While a sense of coherence and compositional harmony saturates every project, the user experience takes these works to the next level.
