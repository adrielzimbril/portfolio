An atmosphere of **harmony, integrity, and serenity** is very prevalent in Japanese web design.

Even if the project boasts an impeccable appearance through extravagant visuals or is driven by ultra-modern features or dynamic effects intended to add a touch of chaos and disrupt harmony, it always finds the perfect balance between content and design. The result is an impression that every element is an integral brick of the composition.

This unity and calm perspective can be linked to the urban understanding of Zen, which establishes a peaceful and relaxed atmosphere. Such projects, with an overall almost ethereal feeling, offer a truly exquisite user experience.

These 20 new examples from talented web designers/developers should give you a clear idea of the inherent characteristics of Japanese websites.

Best Japanese Websites

### Kenji Saito

![Leaving the Film](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356551-Kenji-Saito.jpg)

Kenji Saito showcases the hidden power of HTML5/CSS3/JS to visitors. This talented developer's personal portfolio is filled with fantastic experimental works that can convince anyone that the artist is "like a duck to water" in this field.

### Exit Film

[![Leaving the Film](https://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/2-Exit-Film.jpg)](https://exitfilm.jp/)

Exit Film successfully displays four short videos on users' radar screens in a non-intrusive manner. The elegant, fixed-centered layout combined with a dark, solid background, tiny graphics, and delicate typography creates a strong visual impact while not overwhelming visitors.

### Satoshi Kuroda

![Satoshi Kuroda](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356563-Satoshi-Kuroda.jpg)

The first thing you notice is a stunning brush-style typography that excels beautifully against the background. Not only does the lettering highlight the artist's creative nature, but it also serves as a powerful decorative element that complements the overall appearance.

### Kenji Endo

![Kenji Endo](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356564-Kenji-Endo.jpg)

Kenji Endo's official website skillfully showcases the artist. The elegant black-and-white color scheme enhances the aesthetic, blessing the project with a stunning and memorable look. Additionally, with a few tiny complementary effects, the site exudes positive vibrations.

### Gakuten

![Gakuten](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356575-Gakuten.jpg)

Gakuten is a summer art festival that boldly displays its vibrant personality on its official site, closely linking it to the event. The homepage, as expected, features spectacular photos, team members, and crucial information for those interested in participating.

### Sanga Ryokan

[![Sanga Ryokan](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356576-Sanga-Ryokan.jpg)](https://www.sanga-ryokan.com/)

Sanga Ryokan boasts a beautiful online portfolio with a strong Japanese atmosphere. While the landing page design subtly hints at yin and yang through clever background differentiation, the modular scrolling technique allows users to scan each column individually, beautifully dividing the website into two parts.

### Hys-inc

[![Hys-inc](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356577-Hys-inc.jpg)](https://www.hys-inc.jp/)

Hys-inc opts for a cleaner, purer, and more pristine appearance that certainly catches the eye and easily contrasts with foreground elements. The artist carefully balances graphics, content, and animations to enrich the website and clearly demonstrate their expertise.

### Koto-bana

[![Koto-bana](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356578-Koto-bana.jpg)](https://koto-bana.com/)

If you're looking for pioneering concepts or innovative ideas, explore this site. Koto-bana is a fantastic gallery capable of giving a significant boost to your inspiration. Using modern techniques, it draws flowers from words. It's interesting and captivating.

Lexus

[![Lexus](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/16366356589-Beyond.jpg)](https://discoverlexus.com/stories)

Beyond impresses with its stunning background photo that sets the tone for innovation. The online magazine skillfully combines the serenity of the marine landscape with the luxury of a Lexus vehicle, featuring a truly dramatic image that, like a magnet, draws visitors' eyes.

### Don-gouri

![Don-gouri](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663565810-Don-guri.jpg)

Don-guri offers an excellent way to lose a few minutes. The music-inspired project fills your office atmosphere with positive emotions, brightens the mood, helps you avoid problems, and certainly makes your day better. It charms not only with its original idea but also with its bright and joyful execution.

### Hanatsubaki

[![Howling](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663565912-Howlt.jpg)](https://hanatsubaki.shiseido.com/jp/)

An impressive photo manipulation gives the website a sense of personality and exudes an image of majestic beauty. While personalized photography takes center stage, the perfectly balanced design is based on a harmonious tandem of angular blocks and modest coloring for a strong effect.

### Howlt

[![Howlt](https://designmodo.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/12-Howlt.jpg)](https://howlt.com/archives/artworks)

The designer skillfully blends doodles and animations that easily distinguish the personal portfolio from others and allow it to speak for itself. The cartoon theme highlights the artworks seamlessly and adds to the site's creativity and artistic appeal.

### Fabrique

![Fabrique](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663565913-Fabrik.jpg)

The artist prefers to go straight to the point and showcase fabrics in the best light possible through stunning close-ups. Thanks to clear coloring, a rigid layout, and ample space, the product naturally becomes the focal point.

### Kobaphoto

![Kobaphoto](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566014-Kobaphoto.jpg)

Kobaphoto is a personal portfolio where images speak on behalf of the photographer. The typography and navigation do not distract from the masterpieces, smoothly shifting the focus to the visuals.

### Don-gouri Magazine

![Don-gouri Magazine](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566015-Don-guri-Magazine.jpg)

This is another project by the Don-guru team in our collection that deserves attention. It makes the most of one of the most iconic elements of modern Japanese culture—anime. While it does not include vibrant animations, it is styled in a juicy and distinctive manner that certainly sparks interest.

### Letters

[![Letters](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566116-letters-inc.jpg)](https://www.letters-inc.jp/)

Monochrome and delicate line graphics animated with tiny accompanying effects brighten the homepage of Letters.inc. The resemblance to the grotesque posters of "The Great Gatsby" adds a touch of exquisite beauty to the landing page.

Ondo

![Ondo](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566117-Ondo.jpg)

Ondo features a homepage design that certainly breaks away from the conventional. Unconventional color schemes, whimsical animations, and unique components evoke mixed feelings, capable of leaving a memorable impression on users.

### Midori Aoyama

![Midori Aoyama](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566118-Midori-Aoyama.jpg)

Midori Aoyama has infused her personal portfolio with a high-tech energy. Every element contributes to the theme: delicate typography, ghost buttons, vibrant backgrounds, and even a self-portrait with a beautifully illustrated low-poly touch.

### Herbal Bises

![Herbal Bises](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566119-Herbal-Bises.jpg)

The homepage of Herbal Bises is characterized by an incredible manipulation of photos that reflects an undeniable sense of naturalness. The sumptuous centerpiece not only captures attention but also maintains harmony and enriches the overall design.

### Red Bull

![Red Bull](https://www.adrielzimbril.com/img/picture/163663566220-redbull.jpg)

The artist turns to modern solutions to efficiently manage multimedia, animate content, strengthen branding, and provide an incredible user experience on the website.

**Conclusion**

Japanese artists are completely comfortable with high-end techniques and current trends. While a sense of coherence and harmony in composition saturates each project, the user experience elevates these projects to the next level.
